Making Vehicles Drive Themselves

The Revolution is Coming

Autonomous Vehicles will open up a world of opportunity. We're helping make them a reality.

Varden Labs is a team based out of the University of Waterloo, working on autonomous vehicle technology.

Massive advances in processing power and sensor technology are making it possible for robots to do things we couldn't even dream of a few decades ago. We want to harness this technology to make people's lives better. Improve safety. Save money. Make transportation more efficient.

We are developing and testing autonomous technology from the ground up, showing that a small dedicated team can make vehicles that drive themselves.
