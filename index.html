Free Radical Labs

MAKING VEHICLES DRIVE THEMSELVES

The Revolution is Coming

Autonmous Vehicles will open up a world of opportunity. We're helping make them a reality.

Free Radical Labs is a team based out of the University of Waterloo, working on autonomous vehicle technology.

Massive advances in processing power and sensor technology are making it possible for robots to do things we couldn't even dream of a few decades ago. We want to harness this technology to make people's lives better. Improve safety. Save money. Make transportation more efficient.

We are developping and testing the pieces of this puzzle from the ground up. Showing that a small dedicated team can make vehicles that drive themselves.

The Market

Major auto manufacturers predict that self-driving consumer cars will hit the streets within the decade. We will get to market even sooner by applying autonomous technology to a more lucrative environment, haul trucks. Haul trucks move ore from mines to processing facilities and they present an especially exciting opportunity because they operate in a relatively controlled environment and a single system can improve the efficiency of a $5 million haul truck.

Experts estimate that by not having to stop for shift changes or breaks an autonomous haul truck can perform 20% more work than one driven manually. Automatic haul trucks also increase safety because they never get tired or distracted.
